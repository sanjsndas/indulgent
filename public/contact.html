Frequently Asked Questions

How can I adapt your recipes for specific dietary restrictions?

Most of our recipes include substitution suggestions for common dietary needs like gluten-free, nut-free, or soy-free. For specific questions about adapting a recipe, feel free to contact us with the recipe name and your dietary requirement, and we'll provide tailored suggestions.

Can I submit my own plant-based recipe to be featured on Green Gourmet?

Absolutely! We love discovering new plant-forward recipes from our community. Please use the contact form and select "Collaboration Inquiry" as the subject. Include your recipe, a brief story about it, and any photos you may have. If it aligns with our sustainability values and culinary standards, we'd be thrilled to feature it.

How often do you add new recipes to your collection?

We add new recipes every week, with seasonal collections updated quarterly. Our goal is to continuously expand our 87,000-word recipe library while maintaining our high standards for sustainability, flavor, and culinary innovation. Subscribe to our newsletter to be notified of new additions.

Do you offer cooking classes or workshops?

Yes! We host seasonal virtual cooking workshops focusing on sustainable techniques and plant-forward cuisine. We also occasionally offer in-person classes in the Greenville area. Check our website's events page or contact us to be added to our workshop notification list.

How do you ensure your recipes are truly sustainable?

Each recipe undergoes a sustainability checklist: seasonal ingredient focus, minimal food waste techniques, plant-forward emphasis, and locally-sourced alternatives. We also provide tips for sourcing ingredients sustainably in your area and adapting recipes based on what's available locally.
